Application Context
This application is a comprehensive digital platform designed for the Botswana Nurses Union (BONU) to streamline member management and service delivery. It addresses the challenge of efficiently managing a large membership base and providing diverse services through a unified system. The platform features a Member Portal for self-service registration, profile management, application for various services (e.g., funeral insurance, legal aid, loans, merchandise, electronic contracts), and payment tracking. Concurrently, a Back Office Portal empowers Customer Service Representatives (CSRs) and administrators to review, process, approve, and manage member applications, payments, and service requests, ensuring efficient operations. The application automates membership subscriptions, premium/loan deductions, and order processing, significantly improving operational efficiency and member satisfaction. Expected outcomes include enhanced member engagement, improved access to union benefits, streamlined administrative processes, and robust reporting for informed decision-making, ultimately strengthening the union's support for its members
