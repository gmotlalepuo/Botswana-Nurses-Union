Botswana Nurses Union (BONU) Application Requirements Document
1. Introduction
The Botswana Nurses Union (BONU) intends to develop a digital platform that will allow members to register, manage their memberships, apply for various union services, and make payments online.
The solution will consist of two portals:
1. Customer Portal (Member Portal) Ł Used by BONU members to register, manage their profiles, apply for services, track applications, and make payments.
2. Back Office Portal (CSR Portal) Ł Used by Customer Service Representatives (CSRs), administrators, and management staff to review, approve, process, and manage member applications and services.
2. Customer Portal Requirements
2.1 Member Registration and Authentication
The system shall allow users to:
 Register as BONU members.  Create and manage accounts.  Login using username/email and password.  Reset forgotten passwords.  Update personal information.  Upload supporting documents.  View membership status.  View payment history.

3. Membership Subscription Service
Members shall be able to:
 Apply for BONU membership.  View membership details.  View membership benefits.  Pay monthly membership subscriptions.  Track payment history.  Download membership confirmation letters.
Membership Payments

The system shall:
 Calculate monthly membership subscriptions.  Record payment transactions.  Generate receipts.  Track outstanding balances.  Automatically allocate deductions towards subscribed services.
4. Funeral Insurance Service
The system shall allow members to apply for funeral insurance cover.
Features
 Apply for funeral cover.  Select desired cover level.  Add dependents/beneficiaries.  Upload supporting documents.  View cover status.  View monthly premium amounts.
Premium Deductions
The system shall:
 Calculate funeral cover premiums.  Deduct premiums from the member's monthly membership payment.  Display premium breakdowns.  Track policy status.

6. Loan Application Assistance Service
BONU shall assist members in applying for loans from partnered financial institutions.
Features
Members shall be able to:
 Submit loan requests.  Select preferred bank.  Upload supporting documents.  Track application progress.  View loan approval status.
CSR Processing
CSRs shall:
 Review applications.  Verify documents.  Communicate with financial institutions.  Update application status.  Approve or reject applications.
Loan Repayments
If approved:
 Loan repayment amounts shall be deducted from the member's monthly membership payment.
 The system shall track repayment balances.  Members shall be able to view repayment schedules.
